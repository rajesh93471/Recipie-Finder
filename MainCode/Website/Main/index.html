Why Should We Care?

Food waste isn't just about uneaten food; it has far-reaching consequences that impact our planet, economy, and society. Understanding these consequences is the first step towards taking action.








Environmental Impact

Food waste is a major contributor to greenhouse gas emissions, landfill overflow, and the depletion of natural resources like water and land.

Economic Waste

When food is wasted, so is the money spent on growing, processing, transporting, and selling it.

Social Implications

In a world where many people suffer from hunger, food waste is a stark ethical issue.
