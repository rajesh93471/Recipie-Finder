Save Food, Save Future

Small changes in your daily routine can make a big difference in reducing food waste

Plan Your Meals

Create a weekly meal plan and shopping list. Check your pantry before shopping to avoid buying duplicates. This simple step can reduce your food waste by up to 25%!

Store Food Properly

Learn the optimal storage conditions for different foods. Use airtight containers and organize your fridge with newer items at the back. Proper storage can extend food life by up to 2 weeks!

Use Leftovers Creatively

Transform leftover ingredients into new meals. Learn to repurpose vegetables, meats, and grains into soups, stir-fries, or casseroles. Get creative with your cooking!
